# Quantum Tunneling Simulation Codes

This folder contains the computational codes developed for the paper: 
Montiel-Purdon, F. Y., Villavicencio, J., & Hernández, A. (2025). "Larmor and Delay Times in Transient Quantum Tunneling."
Physica Scripta, 100(6). URL: https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.1088/1402-4896/add220/meta

The probability density solutions and spin expectation values were computed and visualized in `ProbabilityDensitySolution.ipynb` and `SpinExpectationValuesSolution.ipynb` within the considered parameter ranges.

By analyzing the time evolution of these functions, the codes `MoshinskyPeakDensityValue.ipynb` and `SpinPeakExpectationValue.ipynb` were developed to determine the times at which these quantities reach their maximum values. These results are required for the calculation of the Delay Time and the dynamical extension of the characteristic time.

Finally, the results obtained from the previous simulations were analyzed in `DelayTimevsLarmorFrequency.ipynb` to study the dependence of the Delay Time on the Larmor frequency, and in `DynamicalCharacteristicTime.ipynb` to investigate the temporal evolution of the dynamical characteristic time.
